Ragi Puttu with Banana

Ingredients: 1 cup ragi (finger millet) flour 1/2 cup grated coconut A pinch of salt Water, as needed 1 ripe banana, sliced (for serving) Instructions: Prepare the Ragi Flour: If you have store-bought ragi flour, you can use it directly. If not, roast ragi flour in a pan over low heat for a few minutes until you get a nice aroma. Allow it to cool. Make Puttu Mixture: In a mixing bowl, combine the ragi flour, grated coconut, and a pinch of salt. Gradually add water little by little and mix the flour with your fingers until the mixture resembles breadcrumbs. Ensure that the mixture is slightly damp, not too wet. Layering in Puttu Maker: In a puttu maker or steamer, layer the bottom with grated coconut. Add the ragi mixture on top of the coconut layer. Steam the Puttu: Steam the mixture in the puttu maker or steamer for about 10-15 minutes or until the ragi is cooked and has a fluffy texture. Serve: Once done, remove the puttu from the mold and let it cool slightly. Serve the ragi puttu warm, layered with sliced ripe banana.
